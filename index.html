Sneak Peek!

We're taking a design-first approach to creating FLOE. We're focusing on simplicity and ease-of-use, combined with a design philosophy that is inspired by videogames -- first-person shooters in particular. Ever thought of using the entire screen to create your presentations? With FLOE, you can utilize the entire screen and edit your presentation as you would a video. It's designed to be fun, immersive, and easy to use. Cool, right?

Oh, and the best part is: FLOE will be done in HTML5, so it will work on all online devices, such as your mobile phone and your computer! :)
